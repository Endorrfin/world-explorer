## Posting tips (not part of the post)

- **Reach:** LinkedIn tends to suppress posts with an outbound link in the body. To keep the
  link *and* protect reach, you can move the 🔗 line into the **first comment** and replace it
  in the body with "link in the first comment 👇".
- **Media:** a square image or the MP4 takes the most feed space. The **MP4 usually wins** on
  reach (native video). Post the video, or the `-hero.png` if you prefer a still.
- **One language vs two:** the combined UA+EN post is fine; if you want maximum reach in one
  audience, post the UA version and add the EN text as the first comment (or vice-versa).
- Best time for a dev/UA audience: weekday mornings (Kyiv time).
